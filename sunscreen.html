Refillable Mineral Sunscreen

A pharmacy-exclusive, refillable mineral sunscreen designed for daily use, sensitive skin, and environmental responsibility.

Formulated with non-nano mineral UV filters (zinc oxide and titanium dioxide), providing reliable UVA and UVB protection suitable for Singapore’s climate.

Free from unnecessary fragrances and harsh additives. Suitable for all ages and skin types, including sensitive or reactive skin.

Enriched with vitamin E and moisturising emollients to support hydration and skin comfort during sun exposure.

  • Non-nano mineral UV filters
  • Plant-derived squalane and candelilla wax
  • Biodegradable, vegan-friendly ingredients where possible
  • Refill-first model to reduce packaging waste
  • Net weight: 85 g
  • Suitable for: Face & body
  • Skin type: All skin types
  • Texture: Lightweight cream / balm
  • Usage: Daily sun protection

Water, zinc oxide (non-nano), titanium dioxide (non-nano), plant-derived squalane, jojoba oil, lecithins, candelilla wax, silica, tocopherol (vitamin E), radish root extract
